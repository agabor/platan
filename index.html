Simple, Clean and Useable.

Platan is the most easy to use, intuitive personal financing software. You will be surprised how clever it is! It automtically categorizes your bank statement with its in built rules. You will never get lost on our clean and understandable user interface.

Extensible Open Design.

We are proud of our work, thats why we want to show it to you. Platan was written in Qt and C++11. The source code is released unger the GNU General Public License. Moreover Platan is fully extensible with Python, making it easy for our more skilled users to tailor it for their own needs.

Cross-platform.

Software should be available for everyone, no matter what operating system or device they use. We are working hard on makin our software available on the most desktop an mobile devices.
